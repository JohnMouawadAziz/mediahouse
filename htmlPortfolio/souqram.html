SOUQRAM Details

Souqram.net is a free android and iOS online shopping application that allows you to browse and buy online from different products and categories that appears for you based on your location. You can purchase the item through the application with ease, just select the item you want to buy and follow the necessary steps, and then wait for the time to receive the item to your doorsteps. If the item you have received is damaged, defective or not as described on the app, you will receive a full refund or replace the item.
